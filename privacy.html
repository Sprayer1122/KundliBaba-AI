If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use KundliBaba AI.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  1. Service delivery: To calculate your birth chart using Vedic astrology principles and generate your report and answers to your questions using AI-assisted interpretation.
  2. Communication: To send your report, respond to your messages, and provide customer support via WhatsApp or email.
  3. Payment processing: To confirm payment, deliver paid content, and process refund requests when you message REFUND.
  4. Data deletion: To honour deletion requests when you message DELETE or contact support.
  5. Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, and resolve disputes.
  6. Security & fraud prevention: To detect abuse, spam, or fraudulent payment activity.
  7. Service improvement: To improve the quality and accuracy of our readings in aggregate, without using your identifiable data for unrelated advertising.

We do not use your personal data for:

  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Targeted advertising on third-party platforms
  • Profiling for unrelated commercial purposes

Depending on your location, we process your data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: By messaging us on WhatsApp and providing your birth details, you consent to us processing that data to generate your report.
  • Legitimate interests: To operate, secure, and improve our service, prevent fraud, and communicate with you about your account or requests.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Active chat & report data: Retained while you use the service and for up to 12 months after your last interaction, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Deletion requests: Personal data is deleted or anonymised within 30 days of a verified DELETE request. See our Data Deletion Instructions.
  • Payment & transaction records: Retained for up to 7 years as required under Indian tax and accounting laws, but stored separately with limited access.
  • Support emails: Retained for up to 2 years after resolution, unless deletion is requested.
  • Server/security logs: Retained for up to 90 days, then deleted or anonymised.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or irreversibly anonymise it.

  • Right to erasure (deletion): Request deletion of your personal data. Message DELETE in your WhatsApp chat or email support@kundlibaba.in. Full instructions: Data Deletion page.

  • Right to grievance redressal: Raise a complaint with us; we will investigate and respond within 7 business days.

To exercise any right, contact support@kundlibaba.in with the subject line “Privacy Request” and include your WhatsApp number so we can verify your identity.
