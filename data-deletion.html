Overview

At KundliBaba AI, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of the personal information we hold about you.

This page fulfils Meta/Facebook's requirement for a Data Deletion Instructions URL for apps that collect user data through WhatsApp and related platforms.

For full details on what data we collect and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.

What Data We Store About You

When you use KundliBaba AI on WhatsApp, we may store:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name and birth details (date, time, and place of birth)
  • Messages you send in the chat, including your questions and our responses
  • Your kundli report and AI-generated readings
  • Payment status and transaction reference (not your UPI PIN or bank details)

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data using either method below:

Method 1 — WhatsApp (fastest)

  1. Open your existing KundliBaba AI chat on WhatsApp (the same chat where you received your kundli).
  2. Type the word DELETE (capital letters) and send it as a message.
  3. We will confirm your deletion request in the same chat within 1–2 business days.
  4. Your personal data will be permanently deleted or anonymised within 30 days of confirmation.

If you no longer have the chat, use Method 2 below.

Method 2 — Email

  1. Send an email to support@kundlibaba.in.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your WhatsApp phone number (with country code, e.g., +91XXXXXXXXXX) so we can verify your identity and locate your data.
  4. Optionally state: “I request deletion of all my personal data held by KundliBaba AI.”
  5. We will reply within 7 business days confirming your request.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

Once your deletion request is verified and processed:

  • Your name, birth details, chat messages, kundli report, and questions will be permanently deleted from our active systems.
  • Your WhatsApp number will be removed from our customer records.
  • You will no longer receive any messages or reports from KundliBaba AI unless you start a new chat.
  • Backups containing your data will be purged within 30 days as backup cycles complete.

Deletion Timeline

StageTimeline
Acknowledgement of your request Within 1–2 business days (WhatsApp) or 7 business days (email)
Deletion from active databases Within 30 days of verified request
Deletion from backup systems Within 30 days (as backups rotate)

Data We May Retain After Deletion

In certain limited circumstances, we may retain specific data even after a deletion request:

  • Payment and transaction records: Retained for up to 7 years as required by Indian tax, GST, and accounting laws. These records contain only transaction IDs and amounts — not your birth details or chat content.
  • Anonymised/aggregated data: Data that can no longer identify you (e.g., total number of reports generated) may be retained for analytics.
  • Legal holds: Data required to comply with a court order, government request, or ongoing legal dispute.
  • Fraud prevention records: Minimal records related to confirmed fraudulent activity, retained only as long as necessary.

We will never retain your identifiable birth details or chat content after a verified deletion request, except where legally required.

WhatsApp and Meta Platform Data

Deleting your data from KundliBaba AI does not automatically delete your WhatsApp account or message history stored on Meta's servers.

To manage or delete data held by WhatsApp/Meta, refer to:

  • WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • Meta — How to delete your account or data

Correction Before Deletion

If your birth details were entered incorrectly and you want a corrected report instead of deletion, message us in WhatsApp with the correct details or email support@kundlibaba.in. We can regenerate your report without requiring full data deletion.

Questions or Complaints

If you have questions about data deletion, believe your data was not fully deleted, or wish to file a privacy complaint:

  • Email: support@kundlibaba.in
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request / Privacy Complaint
  • Address: KundliBaba AI, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India
  • Privacy Policy: kundlibaba.in/privacy.html

We will investigate and respond within 7 business days.
